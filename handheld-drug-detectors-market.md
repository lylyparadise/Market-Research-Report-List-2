<p><ul><li>IR-spectrometers</li><li>Mass Spectrometry (MS)</li><li>Ion Mobility Spectrometry (IMS)</li><li>Gas Chromatography</li><li>Raman Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld drug detectors utilize various types of technology for detecting illicit substances. IR-spectrometers analyze the interaction between infrared radiation and molecules. Mass Spectrometry (MS) separates ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio. Ion Mobility Spectrometry (IMS) measures the mobility of ions in gas phase. Gas Chromatography separates and detects different substances in a gas mixture. Raman Spectrometry analyzes the scattered light to determine molecular composition. These technologies play a crucial role in enhancing security measures and preventing drug trafficking.</p></p>
