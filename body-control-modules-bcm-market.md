<p><p>Body Control Modules (BCM) are electronic control units that manage various electrical systems in a vehicle, such as lighting, windows, and door locks. There are two main types of BCM market: CAN Body Control Modules and LIN Body Control Modules. CAN BCMs use a Controller Area Network for high-speed communication, making them suitable for complex vehicle systems. LIN BCMs, on the other hand, use Local Interconnect Network for simpler, lower cost applications. Both types play a crucial role in modern automotive electronics.</p></p>
