<p><p>Microchip implants are used for various purposes such as tracking pets, identification, and access control. There are three main types of microchip implants based on their operating frequencies: 125 kHz, 128 kHz, and 134.2 kHz. The 125 kHz microchips are commonly used for animal tracking, while the 128 kHz microchips are used for identification and access control systems. The 134.2 kHz microchips are specifically designed for electronic tagging and tracking of animals for research purposes. Each market type caters to specific needs and applications.</p></p>
