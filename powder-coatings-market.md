<p><ul><li>Thermoset Powder Coating</li><li>Thermoplastic Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder coatings market can be categorized into two main types - Thermoset Powder Coating and Thermoplastic Powder Coating. Thermoset coatings are crosslinked polymers that are cured using heat, providing a more durable and resistant finish. Thermoplastic coatings, on the other hand, can be melted and reformed multiple times without losing their properties. Both types of coatings offer various benefits such as excellent durability, chemical resistance, and environmental advantages, making them popular choices in the coatings industry.</p></p>
