<p><ul><li>Point Sensor</li><li>Fiber Optic Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arc sensors are used in welding processes to detect the presence and characteristics of an arc. There are two main types of arc sensors: Point sensors and Fiber optic sensors.</p><p>Point sensors are traditional arc sensors that are fixed at a specific location near the welding arc to detect its presence and monitor its characteristics. </p><p>Fiber optic sensors use fiber optic cables to transmit signals from the welding arc to a remote location for monitoring and analysis. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and efficiency of welding processes.</p></p>
