<p><p>Automotive plastic fasteners are classified into several types based on their applications in different parts of a vehicle. Interior fasteners are used for components inside the car such as dashboard and seats. Exterior fasteners secure parts like bumpers and headlights. Electronics fasteners are employed in various electronic components within the vehicle. Powertrain fasteners are used in the engine and transmission system. Chassis fasteners are essential for the structural integrity of the vehicle. Wire harnessing fasteners secure wiring and cables throughout the vehicle.</p></p>
