<p><p>Automotive crash sensors market is segmented into various types such as Pressure Sensors, Speed Sensors, Temperature Sensors, Gas Sensors, Level Sensors, and Position Sensors. Pressure sensors are used to measure impact force during crashes. Speed sensors detect rapid deceleration. Temperature sensors monitor changes in temperature due to impact. Gas sensors measure presence of harmful gases post-collision. Level sensors detect changes in fluid levels. Position sensors determine the location of impact. These sensors play a crucial role in ensuring efficient response and safety during automotive accidents.</p></p>
