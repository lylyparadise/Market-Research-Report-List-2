<p><p>Slaughtering equipment market can be categorized into various types such as stunning, killing, cut-up, deboning & skinning, evisceration, and others. Cut-up equipment facilitates the division of carcasses into smaller cuts, while deboning & skinning equipment is used to remove bones and skin. Evisceration equipment helps in removing internal organs. The "Others" category includes additional equipment used in the slaughtering process.</p></p>
