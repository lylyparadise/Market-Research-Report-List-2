<p><ul><li>Single Bond</li><li>Double Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nylon hose clamps are available in two main market types: Single Bond and Double Bond. Single Bond clamps have a single locking mechanism, making them suitable for light to medium-duty applications. On the other hand, Double Bond clamps feature two locking mechanisms, providing extra security and strength, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types offer secure and reliable hose fastening solutions, but Double Bond clamps are preferred for applications that require extra durability and strength.</p></p>
