<p><p>Downdraft range hoods come in two main types - external blower and internal blower. External blower range hoods have the motor and fan located outside the kitchen, which allows for quieter operation and more powerful suction. Internal blower range hoods have the motor and fan housed within the unit itself, making them more compact and easier to install. Both types are effective at removing smoke, grease, and odors from the kitchen while providing a sleek and modern look.</p></p>
