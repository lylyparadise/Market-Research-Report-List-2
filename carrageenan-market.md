<p><strong>The Carrageenan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Iota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carrageenan is a popular food additive used for its thickening, binding, and stabilizing properties. It is available in three main types - Kappa, Iota, and Lambda carrageenan. </p><p>Kappa carrageenan forms strong gels in the presence of calcium ions, making it ideal for dairy and meat products. Iota carrageenan creates soft and elastic gels with potassium salts, commonly used in dairy desserts and beverages. Lambda carrageenan does not gel but acts as a thickener and stabilizer in products like sauces and dressings.</p></p>
