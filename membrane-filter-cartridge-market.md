<p><strong>The Membrane Filter Cartridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyamide</li><li>Polyethersulfone</li><li>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE)</li><li>Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane filter cartridges are commonly classified based on the material used to create the filter. Polyamide cartridges are known for their high chemical resistance and thermal stability. Polyethersulfone cartridges offer excellent bacteria and virus retention. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) cartridges are well-known for their superior chemical compatibility and high temperature resistance. Polypropylene cartridges are cost-effective and widely used for general filtration applications. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific filtration needs of the application.</p></p>
