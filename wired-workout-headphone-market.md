<p><p>The wired workout headphone market can be categorized into two main types: back closed-back and back open-back. Back closed-back headphones provide a sealed design that keeps sound isolated and prevents sound from leaking out. This type is ideal for intense workouts in noisy environments. On the other hand, back open-back headphones have a more open design that allows sound to pass through, creating a more airy and natural sound quality. This type is best suited for low-impact workouts in quiet surroundings.</p></p>
