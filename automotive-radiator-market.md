<p><p>Automotive radiator market types include aluminum and copper radiators. Aluminum radiators are lightweight, durable, and corrosion-resistant, making them popular in modern vehicles. They offer efficient heat dissipation and are often more cost-effective than copper radiators. On the other hand, copper radiators have excellent thermal conductivity, making them efficient at cooling the engine. While they are more expensive than aluminum radiators, they are preferred for high-performance vehicles where heat dissipation is crucial. Both types play a vital role in maintaining the engine's temperature and overall performance of the vehicle.</p></p>
