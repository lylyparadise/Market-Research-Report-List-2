<p><p>Fixed Multi Gas Detectors come in various types namely Catalytic Combustion, Electrochemical (echem), Infrared (IR), and Photoionization Detectors (PID). </p><p>Catalytic Combustion detectors rely on a heated catalyst to detect gases. Electrochemical detectors use a chemical reaction to produce an electrical signal. Infrared detectors measure gas levels by analyzing the absorption of infrared light. Photoionization detectors use ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules and detect their presence. Each type of detector has its own strengths and weaknesses for detecting different gases.</p></p>
