<p><p>Building envelope systems refer to the physical barriers that separate the interior of a building from its exterior surroundings. Types of building envelope systems include liquid coatings, gypsum board, construction glass curtain wall, thermal insulation materials, and other miscellaneous systems. Liquid coatings are applied to surfaces to provide waterproofing and protection. Gypsum board is used for interior walls and ceilings. Construction glass curtain wall systems provide structural support and aesthetic appeal. Thermal insulation materials help regulate temperature and energy efficiency. Other building envelope systems include roofing materials, air barriers, and vapor barriers.</p></p>
