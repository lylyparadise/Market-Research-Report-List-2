<p><p>The Immersion Heater market consists of various types, including Over-the-Side Immersion Heaters, Flanged Immersion Heaters, Screw Plug Immersion Heaters, and others. Over-the-Side Immersion Heaters are designed to be immersed in a tank or container with the heating element suspended over the side. Flanged Immersion Heaters are mounted onto a tank or vessel via a flange. Screw Plug Immersion Heaters are inserted directly into the tank or vessel through a threaded opening. Other types of immersion heaters may include circulation heaters or inline heaters that are designed for specific applications.</p></p>
