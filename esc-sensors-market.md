<p><ul><li>Wheel Speed sensor</li><li>Steering Angle Sensor</li><li>Yaw rate Sensor</li><li>Lateral Acceleration Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ESC sensors play a crucial role in ensuring vehicle stability and safety. The Wheel Speed sensor measures the speed of each wheel and helps prevent skidding. The Steering Angle sensor detects the angle at which the driver is turning the wheel, aiding in stability control. The Yaw rate sensor measures the vehicle's rotation around its vertical axis, while the Lateral Acceleration sensor detects side-to-side movement. These sensors collectively contribute to the effectiveness of Electronic Stability Control systems in maintaining vehicle control during difficult driving conditions.</p></p>
