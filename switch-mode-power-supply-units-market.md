<p><p>Switch-mode power supply units are classified into two main types: AC/DC and DC/DC. AC/DC power supplies convert alternating current (AC) input voltage to direct current (DC) output voltage, making them suitable for powering various electronic devices. On the other hand, DC/DC power supplies convert one DC voltage level to another, allowing for voltage regulation or conversion in applications such as battery-powered devices or automotive electronics. Both types of power supplies offer efficient and reliable power conversion solutions for a wide range of electronic devices.</p></p>
