<p><p>Hi-End Wires Market Types include OFC oxygen free copper, LCOFC copper, PCOCC oxygen-free single crystal copper, and Super PCOCC Copper. OFC copper is known for its high conductivity and low signal loss, making it a popular choice for audio equipment. LCOFC copper offers improved purity levels compared to regular copper wires. PCOCC copper is made of a single crystal structure, further enhancing conductivity. Super PCOCC copper is the highest grade, providing top-tier performance for high-end audio systems.</p></p>
