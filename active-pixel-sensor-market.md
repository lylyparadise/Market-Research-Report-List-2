<p><strong>The Active-pixel Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buried Photodiode-structure</li><li>Selection Transistor</li><li>MOS Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active-pixel sensor market types include buried photodiode-structure, selection transistor, and MOS amplifier designs. Buried photodiode-structure sensors feature photodiodes integrated directly into the pixel wells for improved sensitivity. Selection transistor sensors use a transistor to individually select pixels for readout, allowing for faster data transfer. MOS amplifier sensors incorporate amplifiers within each pixel for enhanced signal quality. These different designs cater to various needs such as sensitivity, speed, and signal amplification in the active-pixel sensor market.</p></p>
