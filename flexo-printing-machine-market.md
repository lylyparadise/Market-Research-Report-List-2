<p><ul><li>Central Impression (CI) Flexo Presses</li><li>Stack Flexo Presses</li><li>In-line Flexo Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexo Printing Machine Market Types include Central Impression (CI) Flexo Presses, Stack Flexo Presses, and In-line Flexo Presses. CI Flexo Presses have a single, large central drum around which the printing units are arranged. Stack Flexo Presses have multiple printing units stacked vertically, allowing for a higher color output. In-line Flexo Presses have printing units arranged in a horizontal line, enabling faster printing speeds. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of productivity, color accuracy, and print quality.</p></p>
