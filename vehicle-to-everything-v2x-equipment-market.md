<p><p>The Vehicle-to-everything (V2X) Equipment market consists of various types of communication technologies. V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle) enables vehicles to communicate with each other to enhance safety and traffic flow. V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure) allows vehicles to communicate with roadside infrastructure such as traffic lights and signs for efficient transportation system. V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian) technology enables vehicles to communicate with pedestrians to prevent accidents. Other markets in this sector include V2N (Vehicle-to-Network) for communication with network services and V2G (Vehicle-to-Grid) for connection with electric grid systems.</p></p>
