<p><p>Ultrasonic fork sensors are used in various industrial applications to detect the presence of objects. There are two main types of Ultrasonic fork sensor markets - NPN and PNP. NPN sensors operate by switching the output to ground when an object is detected, while PNP sensors switch the output to the supply voltage. Both types have their own unique advantages and applications in different industries, but ultimately serve the same purpose of detecting objects accurately and efficiently.</p></p>
