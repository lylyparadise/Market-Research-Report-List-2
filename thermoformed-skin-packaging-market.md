<p><p>Thermoformed skin packaging is available in two main types: Non-Carded Thermoformed Skin Packaging and Carded Thermoformed Skin Packaging. Non-carded packaging involves sealing the product between a plastic film and a paperboard backing, while carded packaging involves placing the product on a cardboard backing and sealing it with a plastic film. Both types offer various benefits such as visibility of the product, protection from external elements, and customizable designs. These packaging solutions are commonly used in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
