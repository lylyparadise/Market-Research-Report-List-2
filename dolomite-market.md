<p><p>Dolomite market can be classified into three main types: calcium dolomite, magnesia dolomite, and others. Calcium dolomite is rich in calcium and is used in various industries like construction, agriculture, and water treatment. Magnesia dolomite has higher magnesium content and is primarily used in the production of magnesium oxide and refractory materials. The "others" category includes dolomite with different chemical compositions and is used in applications such as fertilizers, metal smelting, and glass manufacturing.</p></p>
