<p><p>Pharmaceutical pumps are crucial for various processes in the healthcare industry. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to transfer fluid. Positive displacement pumps work by trapping fluid and pushing it through a system. Jet pumps use an injector to create suction. Air lift pumps rely on air to move fluids. Each type of pump serves a specific purpose in pharmaceutical applications, from transferring medications to mixing compounds. Understanding the different pump types can help ensure efficient and accurate pharmaceutical processes.</p></p>
