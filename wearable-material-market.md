<p><ul><li>Silicones</li><li>Polyurethanes</li><li>Fluoroelastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wearable material market can be divided into three main types: Silicones, Polyurethanes, and Fluoroelastomers. Silicones are preferred for their flexibility and biocompatibility, making them ideal for wearables that come into contact with skin. Polyurethanes offer durability and water resistance, making them suitable for wearables used in harsh environments. Fluoroelastomers provide excellent chemical resistance and high temperature stability, making them suitable for wearables used in industrial settings. Each type of material has unique properties that cater to different needs in the wearable technology market.</p></p>
