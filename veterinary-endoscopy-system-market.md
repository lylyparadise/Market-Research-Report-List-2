<p><p>Veterinary endoscopy systems are classified into three main types: flexible endoscopes, rigid endoscopes, and other endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are long, flexible tubes with a camera attached that can navigate through curved pathways in the body. Rigid endoscopes are straight, rigid tubes with a camera at the end used for more direct and precise procedures. Other endoscopes include specialty endoscopes designed for specific procedures or body parts. Each type of endoscope has its own advantages and applications in veterinary medicine.</p></p>
