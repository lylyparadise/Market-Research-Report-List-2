<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure System</li><li>Vacuum System</li><li>Combination System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic conveyors are categorized into three main types based on their operating systems: positive pressure system, vacuum system, and combination system. Positive pressure systems use compressed air to push materials through the conveyor, while vacuum systems utilize suction to pull materials. Combination systems combine elements of both positive pressure and vacuum systems for efficient material handling. Each system offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications depending on factors such as material type and distance of conveyance.</p></p>
