<p><strong>The Submersible Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Pumps</li><li>Mixed/Radial Flow Pumps</li><li>Peripheral Pumps</li><li>Jet Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submersible centrifugal pumps come in various types such as axial flow pumps, mixed/radial flow pumps, peripheral pumps, and jet pumps. Axial flow pumps are used for high flow, low head applications. Mixed/radial flow pumps are versatile and can handle a wide range of flow rates and pressures. Peripheral pumps are ideal for applications with high pressure and low flow rates. Jet pumps are designed for deep well applications where a suction lift is needed. Each type caters to different pumping needs and specifications in the market.</p></p>
