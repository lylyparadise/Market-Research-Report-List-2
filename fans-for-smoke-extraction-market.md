<p><ul><li>Axial Smoke Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Smoke Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fans for smoke extraction are essential in maintaining safe indoor air quality during fires or other emergencies. Two common types of fans used for smoke extraction are axial smoke fans and centrifugal smoke fans. Axial smoke fans are designed with blades that rotate around an axis, creating high airflow at low pressures, making them ideal for applications in large spaces with low resistance to airflow. Centrifugal smoke fans, on the other hand, use centrifugal force to generate high-pressure airflow, making them suitable for situations where higher pressure is needed to extract smoke efficiently.</p></p>
