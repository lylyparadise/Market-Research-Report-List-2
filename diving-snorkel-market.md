<p><ul><li>Wet</li><li>Semi-dry</li><li>All Dry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diving snorkel market can be categorized into three types: wet snorkels, semi-dry snorkels, and all-dry snorkels. Wet snorkels have the simplest design, with a basic tube attached to a mouthpiece. Semi-dry snorkels feature a splash guard at the top to prevent water from entering while diving. All-dry snorkels have a valve mechanism that closes when submerged, ensuring no water enters the tube. Each type caters to different diving conditions and preferences, providing options for all levels of divers.</p></p>
