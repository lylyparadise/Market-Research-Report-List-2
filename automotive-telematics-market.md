<p><ul><li>Plug and Play Telematics</li><li>Hardwired Install Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plug and Play Telematics refers to a type of automotive telematics system that can be easily installed and removed by simply plugging it into the vehicle's OBD-II port. This type of system is popular among consumers who want a quick and hassle-free way to track their vehicle's performance and location.</p><p>On the other hand, Hardwired Install Telematics involves a more permanent installation process where the system is hardwired into the vehicle's electrical system. This type of telematics is often preferred by fleet managers and commercial vehicle operators who require a more robust and secure solution for monitoring their vehicles.</p></p>
