<p><p>CW Radars and Pulsed Radars are two main types of Long Radar Systems. Continuous Wave (CW) Radars emit a continuous signal allowing for the detection of moving targets. Pulsed Radars, on the other hand, transmit short pulses of radio frequency energy and then listen for echoes. This method allows for accurate range calculations and target detection. Both types of radars have their unique advantages and are used in various applications such as military defense, weather monitoring, and air traffic control.</p></p>
