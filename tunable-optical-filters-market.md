<p><p>Tunable Optical Filters are used in various applications to manipulate light wavelengths. Manual Tunable Optical Filters require manual adjustment by rotating a knob or dial to select the desired wavelength range. Motorized Tunable Optical Filters, on the other hand, can be adjusted remotely or automatically using motors or actuators. Both types offer flexibility in controlling light wavelengths for specific applications, with manual filters being more cost-effective and motorized filters offering greater precision and convenience in adjusting wavelengths.</p></p>
