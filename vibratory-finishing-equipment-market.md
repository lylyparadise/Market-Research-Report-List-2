<p><p>Vibratory finishing equipment is classified into three main types: centrifugal, linear, and CNC. Centrifugal machines utilize a spinning motion to apply force and create a smoother finish on parts. Linear machines move parts along a linear track, subjecting them to abrasive media for polishing. CNC machines are controlled by computer programs to precisely achieve desired finishes on parts. Each type of equipment offers unique advantages and is used in various industries for surface finishing applications.</p></p>
