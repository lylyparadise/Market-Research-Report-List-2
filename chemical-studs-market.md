<p><ul><li>Injectable Adhensive Anchors</li><li>Capsule Adhensive Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemical Studs Market can be categorized into two main types: Injectable Adhesive Anchors and Capsule Adhesive Anchors. Injectable Adhesive Anchors are pre-measured adhesives that come in tubes, allowing for easy application and installation of studs. Capsule Adhesive Anchors consist of a resin and hardener enclosed in a capsule, which is inserted into a pre-drilled hole and then activated to create a strong bond. Both types of adhesive anchors are commonly used in construction for anchoring structural elements to concrete or masonry surfaces.</p></p>
