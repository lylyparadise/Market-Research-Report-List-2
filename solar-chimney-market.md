<p><p>A solar chimney, also known as a solar updraft tower, is a renewable energy technology that harnesses solar energy to create a natural draft for ventilation or energy generation. The concept involves a tall hollow structure with a wide base that absorbs solar heat, causing air to rise and create a draft. This draft can be used to generate electricity through turbines or for passive cooling and ventilation in buildings.</p><p>The Solar Chimney Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12% during the forecast period.
