<p><p>Residential gas generators come in two main types: stationary and portable. Stationary generators are installed permanently outside of a home and are connected to a natural gas or propane supply. They are designed to automatically turn on during power outages to keep essential appliances running. Portable generators, on the other hand, are smaller and can be moved around as needed. They typically run on gasoline or propane and are useful for camping, outdoor activities, or as a backup power source for emergencies.</p></p>
