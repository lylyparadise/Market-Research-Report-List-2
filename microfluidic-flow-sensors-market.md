<p><strong>The Microfluidic Flow Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-contact Type</li><li>Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microfluidic flow sensors have two main types in the market - non-contact type and contact type. The non-contact type sensors utilize external sensors such as optical or thermal sensors to measure the flow rate without physically contacting the fluid. These sensors are ideal for measuring harmful or corrosive fluids. On the other hand, contact type sensors come in direct contact with the fluid to measure the flow rate. These sensors are suitable for measuring a wide range of fluids accurately and provide real-time data for various applications.</p></p>
