<p><p>Solid laser market can be classified into two main types based on the operation mode - Pulse Type and Continuous Type. Pulse Type lasers are designed to emit high-intensity energy in short bursts, making them ideal for applications requiring precise cutting, welding, or drilling. On the other hand, Continuous Type lasers emit a constant beam of energy, making them suitable for continuous operating processes such as engraving, marking, and material processing. Both types of solid lasers cater to different industrial needs and offer distinct advantages in terms of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
