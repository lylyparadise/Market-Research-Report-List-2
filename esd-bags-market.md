<p><p>ESD bags come in various types, including conductive and dissipative polymers, metal, and others. Conductive polymers provide a path for static electricity to flow, while dissipative polymers control the discharge of static electricity. Metal bags offer a shield against electromagnetic interference. Other types may include combination materials or specialized coatings. Each type is designed to protect sensitive electronic components from static electricity and ESD damage, catering to different industries and applications.</p></p>
