<p><p>The Sand Control Equipment market can be categorized into various types such as Gravel Pack, Fracturing Pack, Sand Control Screen, and Others. Gravel Pack equipment is used to prevent sand production by filling the wellbore with gravel to support the formation. Fracturing Pack equipment involves fracturing the formation to create channels for fluid flow while preventing sand production. Sand Control Screen equipment involves installing screens to filter out sand particles. Other types of equipment include chemical treatments and inflow control devices for sand control.</p></p>
