<p><ul><li>Load Type</li><li>Non-Load Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hydrodesulfurization Catalyst Market can be categorized into Load Type and Non-Load Type catalysts. Load Type catalysts contain dispersed metals on a support material, while Non-Load Type catalysts do not have any metal loading. Load Type catalysts are known for their higher activity and selectivity due to the presence of metals, while Non-Load Type catalysts are preferred for their stability and longer lifespan. Both types play a crucial role in the removal of sulfur compounds from various feedstocks in the petroleum refining and chemical industries.</p></p>
