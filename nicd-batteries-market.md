C batteries are slightly smaller and commonly used in medium-drain devices like toys. A batteries are small cylindrical cells often found in remote controls. AA and AAA batteries are the most common types used in a wide range of devices from remote controls to digital cameras. 9-volt batteries are commonly used in smoke detectors and other high-drain devices.
