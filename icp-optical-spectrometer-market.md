<p><p>ICP Optical Spectrometer, also known as Inductively Coupled Plasma Optical Emission Spectrometer, is a powerful analytical instrument that is used for quantitative analysis of metals and non-metals in various samples. It works by vaporizing the sample in an inductively coupled argon plasma and then measuring the intensity of emitted light to determine the concentration of elements present.</p><p>The ICP Optical Spectrometer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.5% during the forecast period.
