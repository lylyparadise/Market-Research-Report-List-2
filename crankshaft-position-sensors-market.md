<p><p>Crankshaft Position Sensors are crucial components in vehicles that track the position and rotation speed of the crankshaft. Linear Position Sensors measure the linear position of the crankshaft, while Rotary Position Sensors detect the rotation angle of the crankshaft. Proximity Sensors use electromagnetic fields to determine the proximity of the crankshaft. Each type of sensor serves a specific purpose in accurately determining the crankshaft's position and rotation, allowing for better engine performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
