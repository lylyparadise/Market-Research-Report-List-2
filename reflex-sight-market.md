<p><ul><li>Cylindrical</li><li>Window</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflex sights come in two main types: cylindrical and window. Cylindrical reflex sights have a cylindrical lens that reflects the reticle onto the target, providing a clear and precise aiming point. Window reflex sights, on the other hand, feature a window through which the shooter can see both the reticle and the target simultaneously, allowing for quick target acquisition and improved accuracy. Both types of reflex sights are popular among shooters for their ease of use and effectiveness in various shooting applications.</p></p>
