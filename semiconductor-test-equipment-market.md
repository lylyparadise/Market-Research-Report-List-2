<p><p>Semiconductor test equipment market can be categorized into two main types: wafer test equipment and packaged device test equipment. Wafer test equipment is used to test the electrical characteristics of individual semiconductor devices on a wafer before they are separated and packaged. Packaged device test equipment, on the other hand, is used to test the final packaged semiconductor devices for functionality and performance. Both types of test equipment are essential in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor products before they are released to the market.</p></p>
