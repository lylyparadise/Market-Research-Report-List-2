<p><p>Linear stepper motors are commonly categorized into two main types: single axis linear stepper motors and dual axis linear stepper motors. Single axis linear stepper motors operate in a linear motion along a single axis, making them suitable for applications requiring straightforward linear movement. On the other hand, dual axis linear stepper motors can move in two perpendicular axes simultaneously, allowing for more complex movements and increased versatility in various industrial applications. Both types offer precision and accuracy in positioning and control.</p></p>
