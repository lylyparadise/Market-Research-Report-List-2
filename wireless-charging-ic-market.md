<p><p>Wireless charging IC market is divided into two main types: Transmitter ICs and Receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs are used in charging pads or docks to transmit power wirelessly to compatible devices. Receiver ICs are integrated into devices such as smartphones or wearables to receive power wirelessly from charging pads. Both types of ICs play a crucial role in enabling the convenient and efficient wireless charging technology, eliminating the need for traditional wired chargers.</p></p>
