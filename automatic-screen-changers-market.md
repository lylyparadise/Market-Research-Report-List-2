<p><p>Automatic screen changers in the market are available in two main types: Single Piston and Double Piston. Single Piston screen changers are equipped with a single hydraulic piston to perform screen changing operations, making them simpler in design and operation. On the other hand, Double Piston screen changers use two hydraulic pistons to enhance the efficiency and speed of screen changing processes, making them suitable for high-volume production environments. Both types offer advantages and can cater to different production needs.</p></p>
