<p><p>Tempered automotive glass is used in various parts of an automobile, including the windscreen, backlite, sidelites, and sunroof. Sidelites are the glass panels on the sides of the car, offering both aesthetic appeal and functionality. The sunroof is a glass panel on the roof of the car that can be opened to let in natural light and fresh air.</p></p>
