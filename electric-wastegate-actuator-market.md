<p><p>Electric wastegate actuators are commonly used in turbocharged engines to control boost pressure. There are two main types of electric wastegate actuators - linear electric wastegate actuators and rotary electric wastegate actuators. </p><p>Linear electric wastegate actuators operate by moving a rod linearly to control the wastegate valve. On the other hand, rotary electric wastegate actuators work by rotating a shaft to control the wastegate valve. Each type of actuator has its own advantages and suitability for specific applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
