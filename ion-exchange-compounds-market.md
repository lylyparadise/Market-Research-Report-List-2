<p><p>Ion exchange compounds are materials that can attract and exchange ions with surrounding solutions. There are two main types of ion exchange compounds: anion exchange compounds and cation exchange compounds. Anion exchange compounds selectively attract and exchange negatively charged ions, while cation exchange compounds attract and exchange positively charged ions. These compounds are widely used in various industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture for applications such as removing contaminants, softening water, and enhancing nutrient uptake in plants.</p></p>
