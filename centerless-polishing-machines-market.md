<p><p>Centerless polishing machines are commonly classified into two main types: Single Head and Multi Head. Single head machines feature a single polishing head that operates independently, making them suitable for smaller scale operations or intricate polishing tasks. On the other hand, multi head machines are equipped with multiple polishing heads, allowing for simultaneous processing of multiple workpieces to increase productivity and efficiency. Both types of machines are used in various industries for achieving high-quality surface finishes on cylindrical workpieces.</p></p>
