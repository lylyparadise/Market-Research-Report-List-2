<p><p>A Power Quality Recorder (PQR) is a device that is used to monitor and analyze the quality of power in electrical systems. It helps in identifying issues such as voltage fluctuations, harmonic distortions, power factor, and other power quality parameters, which can affect the efficiency of electrical equipment. The data recorded by a PQR helps in identifying potential problems, assessing the performance of power distribution systems, and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.</p><p>The Power Quality Recorder Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for reliable power supply in various industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and data centers. The growing focus on energy efficiency and the increasing adoption of renewable energy sources are also driving the market growth.
