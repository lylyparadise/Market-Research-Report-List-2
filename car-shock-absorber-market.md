<p><strong>The Car Shock Absorber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-tube Type & Mono-tube Type (by Structure)</li><li>Hydraulic Type & Pneumatic Type (by Control Method)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car shock absorber market can be classified into two main types based on structure: twin-tube type and mono-tube type. Twin-tube shock absorbers have two tubes, allowing for better heat dissipation and durability. Mono-tube shock absorbers have a single tube for better fluid movement and response. Furthermore, shock absorbers can also be categorized based on control method: hydraulic type and pneumatic type. Hydraulic shock absorbers use fluid to dampen vibrations, while pneumatic shock absorbers use air pressure for suspension control.</p></p>
