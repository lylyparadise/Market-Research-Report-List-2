<p><ul><li>Digital Output</li><li>Analog Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive digitizers are classified into two main types based on their output: digital output and analog output market. Digital output passive digitizers convert input signals into digital data directly, offering precise and accurate tracing capabilities. On the other hand, analog output passive digitizers convert input signals into analog voltage outputs, which can then be interpreted by a separate device. Both types cater to different user preferences and usage scenarios, providing flexibility and versatility in data capturing and processing.</p></p>
