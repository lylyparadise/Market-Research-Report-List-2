<p><ul><li>Hernia Repair</li><li>Hernia Fixation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hernia repair refers to the surgical procedure to fix the hernia by pushing the bulging tissue back into place and reinforcing the weak area with a mesh graft. Hernia fixation, on the other hand, involves securing the mesh in place with sutures, staples, or tissue adhesives to prevent the hernia from reoccurring. Both hernia repair and fixation are crucial in treating hernias and ensuring a successful recovery for patients.</p></p>
