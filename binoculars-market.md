<p><p>Binoculars market consists of two main types: Roof Prism Binoculars and Porro Prism Binoculars. Roof Prism Binoculars are compact and lightweight, with straight barrels that align lenses in a straight line, resulting in a slim design. Porro Prism Binoculars have a wider design with offset lenses for better depth perception and image quality. Both types offer different benefits in terms of portability, image quality, and ease of use, catering to the diverse preferences of consumers in the market.</p></p>
