<p><p>Diesel Lubricity Improver market can be classified into two main types: Acidic Lubricity Improver and Non-Acidic Lubricity Improver. Acidic Lubricity Improvers are chemical additives that work by reacting with the metal surfaces in the engine to reduce friction and wear. Non-Acidic Lubricity Improvers, on the other hand, contain compounds that create a protective film on the metal surfaces to reduce friction. Both types of lubricity improvers are essential in ensuring the smooth operation and longevity of diesel engines.</p></p>
