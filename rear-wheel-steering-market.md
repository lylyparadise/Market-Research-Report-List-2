<p><p>Rear Wheel Steering (RWS) systems are categorized into Passive Steering and Active Steering markets. Passive Steering systems utilize the vehicle's suspension and body movement to steer the rear wheels in the desired direction. Active Steering systems, on the other hand, employ electronic control units to actively steer the rear wheels based on various factors such as speed, steering angle, and road conditions. Both systems aim to improve vehicle stability, maneuverability, and overall driving performance.</p></p>
