<p><p>The IGBT chip market includes three main applications: discrete IGBTs, IGBT modules, and IGBT-intelligent power modules (IGBT-IPM). Discrete IGBTs are individual IGBT chips used for simple switching applications. IGBT modules combine multiple IGBT chips in a single package for higher power applications. IGBT-IPMs integrate IGBT chips with other components like diodes and gate drivers for advanced power control and protection features. Each application serves specific needs in industries such as automotive, industrial, and renewable energy.</p></p>
