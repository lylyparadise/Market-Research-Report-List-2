<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) market can be categorized into four types based on the number of channels: One-channel ABS, Two-channel ABS, Three-channel ABS, and Four-channel ABS. One-channel ABS offers control to one wheel, typically found in motorcycles. Two-channel ABS provides control to two wheels, commonly found in front-wheel-drive vehicles. Three-channel ABS gives control to three wheels, commonly used in older vehicles. Four-channel ABS offers control to all four wheels, providing optimal braking performance and safety in modern vehicles.</p></p>
