<p><p>Sound insulation glass is available in various types to cater to different needs - Hollow Glass, Vacuum Glass, and Laminated Glass. Hollow Glass consists of two or more panes separated by a hollow space filled with gas, providing good sound insulation. Vacuum Glass has a vacuum layer between the panes, which minimizes sound transmission. Laminated Glass has an interlayer that absorbs sound vibrations, therefore increasing sound insulation properties. Each type offers a different level of soundproofing, allowing consumers to choose according to their specific requirements.</p></p>
