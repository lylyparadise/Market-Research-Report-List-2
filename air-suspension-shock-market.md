<p><p>Air suspension shock market includes two main types: Twin Tube Shock Absorbers and Mono Tube Shock Absorbers. </p><p>Twin Tube Shock Absorbers have two tubes - inner and outer, with the inner tube containing the piston while the outer tube contains the hydraulic fluid. These shocks provide a comfortable ride quality and are commonly found in passenger vehicles.</p><p>Mono Tube Shock Absorbers, on the other hand, have a single tube containing both the piston and hydraulic fluid. They offer better heat dissipation and are often used in high-performance and off-road vehicles.</p></p>
