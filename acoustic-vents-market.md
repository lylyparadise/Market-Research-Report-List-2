<p><p>The acoustic vents market can be categorized into three main types: Window Vents, Wall Vents, and Others. Wall Vents, on the other hand, are mounted on walls to provide both ventilation and sound insulation. The category of Others includes vent types that do not fall specifically under window or wall vents, such as floor vents or ceiling vents. These different types of vents cater to varying acoustic and ventilation needs in homes and buildings.</p></p>
