<p><strong>The Omnidirectional AGV Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Guiding</li><li>Magnetic Guiding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Omnidirectional AGVs can be categorized into two main types based on their guiding systems: Optical Guiding and Magnetic Guiding. Optical Guiding AGVs use cameras or sensors to navigate through predefined paths, while Magnetic Guiding AGVs follow magnetic strips or markers placed on the floor. Both types offer precise and efficient navigation capabilities, with Optical Guiding being more flexible in terms of path modification and Magnetic Guiding being more cost-effective and easy to install in existing facilities.</p></p>
