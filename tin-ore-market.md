<p><p>Tin Ore market can be broadly classified into two types: Cassiterite and Yellow Tin Ore. Cassiterite, also known as tin oxide, is the primary source of tin and is commonly found in granite and pegmatite rocks. Yellow Tin Ore, on the other hand, is a secondary source of tin that is usually found in alluvial deposits and is typically associated with weathered granite rocks. Both types of tin ore are important in the production of tin metal, with Cassiterite being the more widely utilized variety.</p></p>
