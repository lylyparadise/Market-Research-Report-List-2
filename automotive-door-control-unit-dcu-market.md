<p><p>Automotive Door Control Unit (DCU) is a crucial component in modern vehicles that controls various functions related to doors and windows. There are different types of DCU available in the market, including Side Door DCU, Power Lift Gate DCU, Anti-trap Window DCU, and Traditional Non-anti-trap Window DCU. Side Door DCU manages the opening and closing of side doors, while Power Lift Gate DCU controls the operation of power lift gates. Anti-trap Window DCU ensures safety by preventing windows from trapping objects or body parts, whereas Traditional Non-anti-trap Window DCU lacks this feature.</p></p>
