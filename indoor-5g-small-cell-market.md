<p><p>Indoor 5G small cell market can be classified into two types: Integrated 5G small cell and Distributed 5G small cell. Integrated 5G small cells combine the baseband unit and radio unit in a single unit, making them compact and easy to deploy in indoor environments. Distributed 5G small cells, on the other hand, separate the baseband unit and radio unit, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability in deployment. Both types cater to the increasing demand for high-speed, low-latency connectivity in indoor spaces such as offices, malls, and stadiums.</p></p>
