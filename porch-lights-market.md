<p><ul><li>Suspension-mounted Porch Lights</li><li>Flush-mounted Porch Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Porch lights are available in two main types: suspension-mounted and flush-mounted. Suspension-mounted porch lights are installed using hanging chains or rods, giving them a more decorative and traditional look. They are often used in covered entryways or on porches with high ceilings. Flush-mounted porch lights are directly attached to the ceiling and provide a more streamlined and modern appearance. They are ideal for covered porches with lower ceilings or for homeowners looking for a simple and practical lighting solution.</p></p>
