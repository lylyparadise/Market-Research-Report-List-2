<p><strong>The PVB Interlayers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Polyvinyl Butyral</li><li>Structural Polyvinyl Butyral</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standard Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) interlayers are commonly used in laminated glass for automotive and architectural applications due to their excellent adhesion and optical clarity. They provide basic safety and security features by holding glass fragments together upon impact. On the other hand, Structural PVB interlayers offer enhanced strength and stiffness, making them ideal for applications where higher security and structural integrity are required, such as in hurricane-resistant windows or structural glass facades. Both types of PVB interlayers play crucial roles in ensuring the safety and durability of laminated glass products.</p></p>
