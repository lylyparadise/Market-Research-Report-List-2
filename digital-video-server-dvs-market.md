<p><p>Digital Video Server (DVS) market types include M-JPEG Technology, MPEG-4 Technology, and H.264 Technology. M-JPEG Technology offers high-quality video compression but requires higher bandwidth. MPEG-4 Technology provides efficient compression while maintaining good video quality and is widely used in video streaming applications. H.264 Technology offers the highest compression efficiency, making it ideal for high-definition video transmission over limited bandwidth networks. Each technology caters to different needs and preferences in the digital video server market.</p></p>
