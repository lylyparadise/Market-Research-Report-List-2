<p><p>Industrial centralised vacuum systems are divided into two main types: cyclonic central vacuum systems and filtered central vacuum systems. Cyclonic central vacuum systems use centrifugal force to separate dirt and debris from the air, while filtered central vacuum systems use filters to trap particles before releasing clean air. Both types offer efficient cleaning solutions for industrial settings, but the choice between them depends on factors such as the type of debris being collected and the desired level of filtration.</p></p>
