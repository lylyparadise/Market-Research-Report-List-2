<p><p>Automated Blood Analyzer Market consists of different types such as Two-part Differential, Three-part Differential, and Five-part Differential analyzers. Two-part Differential analyzers provide basic information on the number and percentage of different types of white blood cells. Three-part Differential analyzers offer more detailed information on the types of white blood cells present. Five-part Differential analyzers provide the most comprehensive analysis by differentiating between various subtypes of white blood cells. Each type of analyzer offers varying levels of detail and insight into a patient's health status.</p></p>
