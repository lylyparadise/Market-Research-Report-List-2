<p><strong>The Cold Rolled Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Pipe</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold Rolled Plate Market includes various types such as Spiral Weld Pipe, Electric Resistance Weld, and Double Submerged Arc Weld. Spiral Weld Pipe involves rolling a strip of steel into a spiral shape and welding along the seam. Electric Resistance Weld utilizes electricity to create heat and join the edges of the steel together. Double Submerged Arc Weld involves submerging the steel in a flux material while welding to protect the weld from contamination. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different applications within the cold rolled plate market.</p></p>
