<p><p>Surgical wear market can be categorized into two main types: Disposable wear and Reusable wear. Disposable wear includes items such as surgical masks, gowns, and caps that are designed for single-use and are discarded after each procedure to prevent cross-contamination. Reusable wear, on the other hand, includes items like surgical scrubs and lab coats that can be laundered and used multiple times. Both types of surgical wear play a crucial role in maintaining a sterile environment and protecting healthcare workers and patients during surgical procedures.</p></p>
