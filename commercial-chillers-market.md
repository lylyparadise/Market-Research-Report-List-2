<p><strong>The Commercial Chillers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Chillers</li><li>Scroll Chillers</li><li>Screw Chillers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial chillers are commonly categorized into four types in the market: centrifugal chillers, scroll chillers, screw chillers, and other chillers. Centrifugal chillers utilize the principle of centrifugal force to cool water or other fluids. Scroll chillers operate by compressing refrigerant between two scrolls. Screw chillers use rotary screw compressors to create chilled water. Other market chillers include absorption chillers, which use heat to drive the cooling process, and reciprocating chillers which use a piston driven by an electric motor to compress refrigerant.</p></p>
