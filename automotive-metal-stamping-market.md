<p><ul><li>Blanking</li><li>Embossing</li><li>Bending</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive metal stamping is a process used to manufacture metal parts for vehicles. Blanking involves cutting out a flat shape from a metal sheet, while embossing creates raised designs on the metal surface. Bending is a technique used to shape the metal into desired forms. These three types of metal stamping methods are commonly used in the automotive industry to produce various components such as body panels, chassis parts, and structural components. Each method plays a crucial role in manufacturing high-quality, precise metal parts for vehicles.</p></p>
