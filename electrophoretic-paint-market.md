<p><ul><li>Anodic Electrophoretic Paint</li><li>Cathodic Electrophoretic Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrophoretic paint market can be divided into two main types: Anodic Electrophoretic Paint and Cathodic Electrophoretic Paint. </p><p>Anodic Electrophoretic Paint involves positively charged paint particles being attracted to the negatively charged metal substrate, forming a durable coating primarily used in automotive applications.</p><p>On the other hand, Cathodic Electrophoretic Paint utilizes negatively charged paint particles attracted to positively charged metal substrate, providing excellent corrosion resistance and frequently used in industrial equipment and household appliances.</p></p>
