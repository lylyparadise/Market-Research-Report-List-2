<p><p>Liquid Sodium Silicate (LSS) Market is divided into three main types: LSS A, LSS B, and LSS C. LSS A is characterized by a high SiO2/Na2O ratio and is used in detergents, catalysts, and zeolites. LSS B has a lower SiO2/Na2O ratio and finds applications in coatings, adhesives, and construction materials. LSS C has an intermediate ratio and is commonly used in water treatment, agriculture, and paper manufacturing. Each type of LSS serves specific industries and applications based on their composition and properties.</p></p>
