HDPE recycling involves the recycling of high-density polyethylene bottles, typically used for milk jugs and detergent containers. The "others" market includes the recycling of various types of plastic bottles, such as PVC and LDPE. Each segment plays a crucial role in reducing plastic waste and promoting sustainability.</p></p>
