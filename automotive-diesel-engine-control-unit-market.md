<p><strong>The Automotive Diesel Engine Control Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump Control Unit</li><li>Rail Control Unit</li><li>Injector Control Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Diesel Engine Control Unit market consists of three main types: Pump Control Unit, Rail Control Unit, and Injector Control Unit. The Pump Control Unit manages the fuel injection system, regulating the fuel flow rate and pressure. The Rail Control Unit controls the fuel rail pressure to ensure proper fuel distribution to the injectors. The Injector Control Unit controls the timing and duration of fuel injection into the combustion chamber. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing the performance and efficiency of diesel engines.</p></p>
