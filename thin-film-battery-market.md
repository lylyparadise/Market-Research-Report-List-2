<p><p>A thin film battery is a type of solid-state battery that utilizes thin films of various materials, such as lithium, to store and release energy. The increasing demand for compact and flexible power sources in various applications, such as wearable devices, Internet of Things (IoT) devices, and medical devices, is driving the growth of the thin film battery market.
