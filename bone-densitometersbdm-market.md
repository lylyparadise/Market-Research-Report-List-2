<p><p>Bone Densitometers(BDM) are medical devices used to measure bone density and assess the risk of fractures due to osteoporosis. There are two main types of BDM: Axial BDM and Peripheral BDM. Axial BDM is used to measure bone density in the spine and hip, while Peripheral BDM is used to assess bone density in peripheral regions such as the wrist, heel, or finger. Both types of BDM are important tools in diagnosing and monitoring bone health to prevent fractures and osteoporosis-related complications.</p></p>
