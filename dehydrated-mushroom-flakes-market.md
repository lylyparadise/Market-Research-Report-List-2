<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dehydrated Mushroom Flakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated mushroom flakes market can be divided into two main types: air drying and freeze drying. </p><p>Air drying involves exposing the mushrooms to hot air or sunlight until the moisture content is reduced, resulting in shriveled flakes with a concentrated flavor. This method is cost-effective and commonly used for preserving mushrooms.</p><p>Freeze drying, on the other hand, involves freezing the mushrooms and removing the moisture through sublimation. This process preserves the shape, color, and flavor of the mushrooms better than air drying but is more expensive.</p></p>
