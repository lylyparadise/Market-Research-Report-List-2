<p><strong>The Blister Packaging Tooling Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoform</li><li>Cold-form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blister packaging tooling for thermoform applications involves using heat to shape a plastic sheet around a mold, creating a cavity for the product. This method is commonly used for packaging consumer goods such as electronics and pharmaceuticals. Cold-form blister packaging tooling, on the other hand, involves forming cavities using pressure in a cold state. This method is often used for packaging pharmaceutical products that are sensitive to heat. Both thermoform and cold-form blister packaging tooling are essential in the packaging industry for protecting products during transportation and display.</p></p>
