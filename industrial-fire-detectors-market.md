<p><p>Industrial fire detectors market types include heat detector type, ionization detector type, photoelectric detector type, and ionization/photoelectric detector type. Heat detectors sense changes in temperature, while ionization detectors detect smoke particles, and photoelectric detectors detect visible smoke particles. Ionization/photoelectric detectors combine both technologies for enhanced fire detection. Additionally, there are other types of fire detectors such as aspirating smoke detectors, flame detectors, and beam detectors that cater to specific industrial applications and environments. Each type offers unique features and benefits to suit different fire detection needs.</p></p>
