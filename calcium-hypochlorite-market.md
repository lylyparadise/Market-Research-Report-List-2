<p><p>Calcium Hypochlorite Market is classified into two types: Calcium Process and Sodium Process. The Calcium Process involves the use of calcium hydroxide and chlorine gas to produce calcium hypochlorite, while the Sodium Process uses sodium hydroxide and chlorine gas. The Calcium Process is known for its higher available chlorine content and longer shelf life, making it preferred for water treatment applications. On the other hand, the Sodium Process is cost-effective and widely used in industries like agriculture and cleaning products.</p></p>
