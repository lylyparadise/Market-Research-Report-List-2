Power semiconductor devices are standalone devices like diodes and transistors used for power electronics applications. Power modules are packages that contain multiple power semiconductor devices, making them easier to use in larger systems. Power integrated circuits combine power semiconductor devices with control circuitry on a single chip, offering compact and efficient solutions for power management in various applications.
