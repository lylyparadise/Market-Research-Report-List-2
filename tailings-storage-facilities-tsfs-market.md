<p><p>Tailings Storage Facilities (TSFs) are essential for managing mining waste, with different types catering to specific needs. Dry stacking TSFs involve stacking filtered tailings on land, resulting in a smaller footprint and reduced water usage. Underground storage TSFs utilize natural or artificial cavities to store tailings, reducing surface disturbance. Other types include in-pit storage and subaqueous disposal methods. Each type offers unique benefits and challenges, allowing mining companies to choose the most suitable option based on their operational requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
