<p><p>Thermopile IR sensors are available in two main market types: Through Hole and Surface Mount Device (SMD). Through Hole sensors are mounted by inserting their leads through holes in a circuit board and soldering them in place, providing a strong mechanical connection. SMD sensors, on the other hand, are mounted directly onto the surface of the circuit board, saving space and allowing for automated assembly. Both types offer unique advantages and are commonly used in various applications requiring accurate temperature measurements and infrared sensing capabilities.</p></p>
