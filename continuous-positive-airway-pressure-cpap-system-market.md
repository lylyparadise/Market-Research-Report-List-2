<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) System market includes two main types: Automatic CPAP and Non-automatic CPAP. Automatic CPAP machines adjust air pressure levels based on the individual's breathing patterns, providing a more customized and comfortable experience. On the other hand, Non-automatic CPAP machines deliver a constant air pressure throughout the night without adjusting to changes in breathing. Both types of CPAP systems are effective in treating obstructive sleep apnea by keeping the airways open and allowing for uninterrupted breathing during sleep.</p></p>
