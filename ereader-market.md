<p><p>The eReader market can be broadly categorized into two types based on the display technology used: E-ink and LCD. E-ink readers utilize electronic paper technology to create a print-like reading experience with minimal eye strain, longer battery life, and high visibility in sunlight. On the other hand, LCD readers have backlit screens that offer color display, multimedia capabilities, and faster refresh rates for smoother animations. While E-ink devices are favored for reading books and documents, LCD devices are preferred for multimedia content and interactive features.</p></p>
