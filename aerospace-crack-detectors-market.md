<p><p>Aerospace Crack Detectors Market consists of various types such as Eddy Current, Dielectric, Imaging, and Phased Array. Eddy Current detectors use electromagnetic induction to detect surface cracks. Dielectric detectors measure changes in electrical properties to identify cracks. Imaging detectors use advanced imaging technology to visualize cracks for accurate inspection. Phased Array detectors use multiple ultrasonic elements to scan and detect cracks in aerospace components. Each type offers unique advantages in detecting and monitoring cracks in aerospace materials and structures.</p></p>
