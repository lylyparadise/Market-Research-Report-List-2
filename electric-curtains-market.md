<p><p>The electric curtains market comprises four main types: blinds, shades, shutters, and drapery. Blinds are vertical or horizontal slats that can be tilted or drawn open. Shades are made of fabric or woven material that can be rolled up or folded. Shutters are solid panels that swing open or fold back. Drapery refers to heavy fabric curtains that hang from a rod and can be opened or closed manually or electronically. Each type offers different styles and functionalities to suit varying preferences and home decor aesthetics.</p></p>
