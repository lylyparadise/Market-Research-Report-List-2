<p><p>Modelling clay can be categorized into two main types: non-dry clay and air-dry clay. Non-dry clay requires baking or firing in a kiln to set and harden, making it a popular choice for professional artists and sculptors. On the other hand, air-dry clay hardens on its own when exposed to air, making it a convenient option for beginners and children. Each type of clay caters to different markets based on their specific needs and preferences, offering a wide range of possibilities for artistic creations.</p></p>
