<p><p>RF Power Meter is a device used to measure the power of radio frequency signals. There are two main types of RF Power Meters: Absorption Type and Through Type. Absorption Type Power Meters measure power by absorbing it, while Through Type Power Meters measure power passing through them. Both types are commonly used in various applications such as telecommunications, broadcasting, and military. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the power measurement task at hand.</p></p>
