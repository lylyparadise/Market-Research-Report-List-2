<p><ul><li>Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Image Intensifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CBCT Dental Imaging Market consists of two main types of detectors: Flat Panel Detectors and Image Intensifiers. Flat Panel Detectors use a thin, flat panel composed of arrays of pixels to capture X-ray images with high resolution and efficiency. On the other hand, Image Intensifiers amplify X-ray images using a series of lenses and a photoelectric screen. Each type offers distinct advantages and is used in various dental imaging applications based on factors such as image quality, cost, and performance.</p></p>
