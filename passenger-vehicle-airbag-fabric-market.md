<p><ul><li>Flat Airbag Fabric</li><li>OPW Airbag Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger vehicle airbag fabric is available in two primary types: flat airbag fabric and OPW (one-piece woven) airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is made of multiple layers of fabric that are sewn together, providing flexibility and protection upon inflation. OPW airbag fabric, on the other hand, is made of a single piece of woven fabric, offering a lightweight and durable solution for airbag deployment. Both types of airbag fabrics are designed to enhance safety and protect passengers in the event of a collision.</p></p>
