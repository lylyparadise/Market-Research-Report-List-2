<p><p>The UAV jammer market can be categorized into three main types: ground-based, hand-held, and UAV-based. Ground-based jammers are stationary devices used to disrupt the communication signals of UAVs in a specific area. Hand-held jammers are portable devices that can be carried by individuals to target UAVs in close proximity. UAV-based jammers are mounted on drones to interfere with the operation of other UAVs. Each type of jammer offers unique advantages and applications in the field of counter-drone technology.</p></p>
