<p><p>Asphalt additives can be classified into various types based on their functions and applications. These include asphalt modifiers, which enhance the performance and durability of asphalt pavements. Antistripping agents help improve the adhesion between asphalt and aggregates. Asphalt emulsifiers are used to create stable emulsions for asphalt applications. Surfactant additives lower surface tension and improve mixing properties. Foam stabilizers help in producing high-quality foamed asphalt mixtures. Additionally, there are other additives available in the market that cater to specific requirements of asphalt production and construction.</p></p>
