<p><p>Electrotherapy market includes various types such as Extracorporeal Shock Wave Therapy, Interferential Therapy, Magnetic Field Therapy, Ultrasound Therapy, Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) Therapy, and others. Extracorporeal Shock Wave Therapy uses high-energy shock waves to treat chronic pain. Interferential Therapy involves using low-frequency electrical currents to reduce pain and improve circulation. Magnetic Field Therapy uses magnetic fields to alleviate pain and promote healing. Ultrasound Therapy uses high-frequency sound waves to treat muscle and joint pain. TENS Therapy involves using electrical stimulation to relieve pain. Other types of electrotherapy include various innovative techniques for pain management.</p></p>
