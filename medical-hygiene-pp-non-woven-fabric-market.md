<p><p>Medical hygiene PP non woven fabric market is segmented into four types based on the manufacturing process: drylaid, airlaid, wetlaid, and spunlaid. Drylaid process involves laying fibers directly on a conveyor belt, while airlaid process uses air to create a web of fibers. Wetlaid process involves forming a fiber suspension in water before laying on a conveyor belt. Spunlaid process uses continuous filaments spun from polymers. Each process has its own advantages and applications in the medical hygiene industry.</p></p>
