<p><p>Soil Remediation market includes various methods to treat contaminated soil. Bioremediation utilizes microorganisms to break down pollutants. Chemical oxidization involves injecting chemicals to break down contaminants. Stabilization/solidification combines additives to reduce the mobility of contaminants. Soil washing involves separating contaminants from soil using water and chemicals. Thermal desorption uses high temperatures to vaporize and separate contaminants from soil. Each method has its own advantages and is used depending on the type and extent of contamination present in the soil.</p></p>
