<p><p>Analog signal boosters are older technology that amplify the existing cell phone signal using an antenna and amplifier. Smart signal boosters, on the other hand, are more advanced and use modern technology to automatically adjust and optimize the signal for the best performance. They often come with features like remote monitoring and control through smartphones. Both types of boosters aim to improve cell phone signal strength and quality for better call quality and faster data speeds.</p></p>
