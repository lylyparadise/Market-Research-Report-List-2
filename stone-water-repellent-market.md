<p><p>Stone water repellent market can be divided into two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based repellents are made with chemicals dissolved in a solvent and are typically more durable and long-lasting. On the other hand, water-based repellents are environmentally friendly and easier to apply, but may not be as long-lasting as solvent-based options. Both types of repellents help prevent water damage and staining on a variety of stones, such as marble, granite, and limestone.</p></p>
