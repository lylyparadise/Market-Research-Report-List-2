<p><p>Forklift seats come in two main types: mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats provide support through springs and shocks, adjusting to the operator's weight and movements. Air suspension seats, on the other hand, use airbags to provide a smooth and comfortable ride by automatically adjusting to the operator's weight and terrain conditions. Both types aim to reduce operator fatigue and increase productivity by providing a more ergonomic and comfortable seating experience in forklifts.</p></p>
