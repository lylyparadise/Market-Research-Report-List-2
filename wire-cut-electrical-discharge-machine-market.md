<p><p>Wire-Cut Electrical Discharge Machines are classified into two main types based on their flushing method: Wire-Cut Flushing Type and Wire-Cut Submerge Type. </p><p>Wire-Cut Flushing Type machines use a flushing method to remove debris and cool the workpiece during the cutting process. On the other hand, Wire-Cut Submerge Type machines submerge the workpiece in dielectric fluid to prevent sparking and improve cutting precision. Both types of machines offer unique benefits and are used in various industrial applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
