<p><strong>The Electric Heat Tracing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Regulating Wattage</li><li>Constant Wattage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric heat tracing systems are used for maintaining the temperature of pipes and equipment in various industries. There are two main types of heat tracing systems: self regulating wattage and constant wattage. Self regulating wattage systems automatically adjust their heat output based on the temperature, making them more energy efficient and reliable. Constant wattage systems provide consistent heat output regardless of temperature changes, making them simpler to install and control. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications based on specific heating requirements.</p></p>
