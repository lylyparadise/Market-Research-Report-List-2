<p><strong>The Microwave Absorber Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Permeability</li><li>High Permeability</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microwave absorber material market types can be categorized into low permeability and high permeability materials. Low permeability materials have the ability to absorb microwave energy effectively, minimizing reflection and transmission. They are suitable for use in applications where high absorption of microwave energy is required. On the other hand, high permeability materials have a higher ability to transmit microwave energy, making them ideal for applications where absorption is not the primary focus, such as in wireless communication systems.</p></p>
