<p><ul><li>Solid Body (Chip) Fuses</li><li>Wire-in-Air Fuses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive SMD fuses are available in two main types: Solid Body (Chip) Fuses and Wire-in-Air Fuses. Solid Body Fuses are compact in size and made of ceramic, providing protection against overcurrent conditions. They are commonly used in modern automotive electronics. Wire-in-Air Fuses have a wire element enclosed in a ceramic housing and are designed to disconnect quickly in case of a fault. Both types of fuses are essential in protecting electrical circuits in vehicles from damage due to overcurrent.</p></p>
