<p><p>Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) wafers are primarily categorized into three main types based on the growth technique used: LEC (Liquid Encapsulated Czochralski) Grown GaAs, VGF (Vertical Gradient Freeze) Grown GaAs, and Other market types. LEC Grown GaAs wafers have high purity and crystalline quality, making them suitable for high-performance devices. VGF Grown GaAs wafers offer better control over the crystal growth process, resulting in improved uniformity and scalability. Other market types include specialty GaAs wafers tailored for specific applications or developed using innovative growth methods.</p></p>
