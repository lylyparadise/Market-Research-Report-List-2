<p><ul><li>Colorimetric</li><li>Fluorometric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lipid peroxidation assay kits are available in two main types: colorimetric and fluorometric. Colorimetric kits measure lipid peroxidation by detecting a color change, which can be quantified with a spectrophotometer. Fluorometric kits utilize a fluorescent dye that emits light when reacting with lipid peroxidation products, which can be measured with a fluorometer. Both types of kits are commonly used in research and pharmaceutical industries to study oxidative stress and lipid damage in cells and tissues.</p></p>
