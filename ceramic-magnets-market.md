<p><p>Ceramic magnets are divided into two main types - Permanent Ceramic Magnets and Soft Ceramic Magnets. Permanent Ceramic Magnets are commonly used in various applications due to their high resistance to corrosion and demagnetization. They are known for their strong magnetic properties and long-lasting durability. On the other hand, Soft Ceramic Magnets have lower coercivity and are easily magnetized and demagnetized. They are typically used in applications that require temporary magnetism or frequent changes in magnetic properties. Both types play significant roles in different industries and applications.</p></p>
