<p><p>The mosquito repellent market includes various types of products such as coils, vaporizers, mats, aerosols, and creams. Coils are spiral-shaped repellents that release smoke when burned. Vaporizers are devices that heat up repellent liquids to create vapor. Mats are small pads soaked in repellent that are placed in devices to release fumes. Aerosols are spray repellents for direct application. Creams are topical products that are applied to the skin as a barrier against mosquitoes. Each type provides a different method of repelling mosquitoes effectively.</p></p>
