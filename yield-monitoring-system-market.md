<p><p>Yield monitoring systems in the agriculture industry come in two main types: guidance systems and remote sensing systems. Guidance systems help farmers optimize their planting and harvesting by providing real-time data on crop health and yield, allowing for precise adjustments to maximize productivity. Both types of systems are essential tools in modern agriculture for improving efficiency and maximizing yields.</p></p>
