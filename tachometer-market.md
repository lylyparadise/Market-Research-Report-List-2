<p><p>Tachometers are instruments used to measure the rotational speed of a shaft or disk in machinery. They can be categorized into two main types based on how they measure speed. Time measurement tachometers calculate speed by measuring the time it takes for a shaft to complete one revolution, while frequency measurement tachometers calculate speed based on the frequency of electrical pulses generated by the rotating shaft. Both types of tachometers are commonly used in various industries for monitoring and controlling the speed of machinery.</p></p>
