<p><strong>The Automotive Motor Igniting Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Magnetic Circuit Type Igniting Coil</li><li>Closed Magnetic Circuit Type Igniting Coil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive motor igniting coils can be categorized into two main types based on their magnetic circuit design: open magnetic circuit type and closed magnetic circuit type. </p><p>The open magnetic circuit type igniting coil has a gap between the core and the armature, allowing for a more efficient magnetic field transfer. </p><p>On the other hand, the closed magnetic circuit type igniting coil has a continuous magnetic path between the core and the armature, providing higher energy efficiency and better spark performance. Both types have their advantages and are used in various automotive applications.</p></p>
