<p><p>The broadband market comprises various types of technologies available for telecommunications services, including cable, DSL (digital subscriber line), FTTP (fiber-to-the-premises), Wi-Fi, and WiMAX. Cable broadband utilizes coaxial cables to transmit data, while DSL uses telephone lines. FTTP delivers data using fiber-optic cables directly to the premises. Wi-Fi provides wireless connectivity within a specific area, while WiMAX offers wireless broadband access over a wider range. Each technology has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
