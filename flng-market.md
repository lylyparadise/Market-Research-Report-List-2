<p><strong>The FLNG Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LNG FPSO</li><li>FSRU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FLNG stands for Floating Liquefied Natural Gas, which includes two main market types: LNG FPSO (Floating Production, Storage, and Offloading) and FSRU (Floating Storage and Regasification Unit). LNG FPSOs are vessels that produce, store, and offload LNG, while FSRUs are floating facilities used for storing and regasifying LNG before supplying it to onshore facilities. Both market types enable the transportation and processing of LNG in offshore locations, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness in natural gas production and distribution.</p></p>
