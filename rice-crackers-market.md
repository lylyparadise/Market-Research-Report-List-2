<p><p>Rice crackers come in two main types based on the type of rice used: Non-Glutinous Rice Base and Glutinous Rice Base. Non-Glutinous Rice Base crackers are made from regular rice varieties and are crispy and light in texture. They are popular for their versatile flavors and can be enjoyed as a snack or with dips. Glutinous Rice Base crackers, on the other hand, are made from sticky rice and have a chewier texture. They are often sweetened and are commonly found in Asian desserts or served as a treat on their own.</p></p>
