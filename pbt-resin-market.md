<p><p>PBT Resin is primarily categorized into two market types: Injection Grade PBT and Extrusion Grade PBT. Injection Grade PBT is specifically designed for injection molding applications, offering high strength, rigidity, and excellent surface finish. On the other hand, Extrusion Grade PBT is tailored for extrusion processes, providing good heat resistance, dimensional stability, and chemical resistance. Both market types cater to specific manufacturing needs and play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, electrical, and consumer goods.</p></p>
