<p><p>Film dielectric capacitors can be categorized into two main types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed to work with alternating current circuits, providing stable performance and high capacitance. On the other hand, DC film capacitors are specifically designed for direct current applications, offering low leakage currents and high reliability. Both types of film capacitors are widely used in various electronic devices and equipment for energy storage and filtering purposes.</p></p>
