<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Clutches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Friction Clutches</li><li>Electromagnetic Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle clutches are essential components in the proper functioning of commercial vehicles. There are two main types of commercial vehicle clutches - Friction Clutches and Electromagnetic Clutches. Friction clutches use friction to transmit power between the engine and transmission, while electromagnetic clutches use electromagnetic force to engage and disengage the clutch. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient operation of commercial vehicles, making them vital components in the automotive industry.</p></p>
