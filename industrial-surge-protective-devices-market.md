<p><p>Industrial Surge Protective Devices market is divided into four types - Type 1, Type 2, Type 3, and Type 4. Type 1 devices offer protection from direct lightning strikes and are used at the service entrance. Type 2 devices protect against high-energy surges and are installed at the distribution panel. Type 3 devices shield sensitive equipment from transient overvoltages and are placed close to the equipment. Type 4 devices provide protection for specific applications and are tailored to meet the requirements of individual devices.</p></p>
