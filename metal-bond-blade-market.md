<p><p>Metal bond blades are used in cutting hard materials like granite, concrete, and ceramics. They come in different types based on the grit material used in the bond. SD grit blades use synthetic diamonds for cutting, SDC grit blades use a combination of synthetic diamonds and cubic boron nitride for added durability, and CBN grit blades exclusively use cubic boron nitride. Each type offers different cutting characteristics and durability, catering to specific cutting needs and material hardness levels.</p></p>
