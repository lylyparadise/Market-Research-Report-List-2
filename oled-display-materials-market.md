<p><strong>The OLED Display Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emitting Layer (EML)</li><li>Hole Transport Layer (HTL)</li><li>Electron Transport Layer (ETL)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OLED display materials market can be categorized based on their functions within the display technology. The Emitting Layer (EML) materials are responsible for emitting light, the Hole Transport Layer (HTL) materials facilitate the movement of positive charges, the Electron Transport Layer (ETL) materials aid in the movement of negative charges. Other materials in the market may include conductive, protective, or encapsulation layers. These different types of materials play crucial roles in the functioning of OLED displays.</p></p>
