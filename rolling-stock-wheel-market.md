<p><strong>The Rolling Stock Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rolling stock wheels can be classified into two main types, casting and forged. Casting involves molten metal being poured into a mold to create the wheel shape, while forged wheels are made by shaping heated metal through a series of compressive forces. Casting wheels are often more cost-effective and suitable for mass production, while forged wheels are known for their superior strength and durability. Both types have their own benefits and are used in different applications within the rolling stock industry.</p></p>
