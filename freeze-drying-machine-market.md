<p><p>A Freeze Drying Machine is a device that removes moisture from a product using a process called freeze-drying. Freeze drying machines are widely used in the production of dried fruits, vegetables, meats, and instant coffee, among others.</p><p>The Freeze Drying Machine Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period.
