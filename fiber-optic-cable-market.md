<p><p>Fiber optic cable market can be divided into two main types: single-mode fiber optic cable and multi-mode fiber optic cable. Single-mode fiber optic cable is designed for long-distance transmission with a single ray of light, making it ideal for telecommunications and data centers. On the other hand, multi-mode fiber optic cable is used for shorter distances and multiple rays of light, making it suitable for local area networks and campus environments. Both types have their unique applications and advantages in the market.</p></p>
