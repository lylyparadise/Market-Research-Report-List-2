<p><ul><li>Straight Guide Wire</li><li>Angled Guide Wire</li><li>J-Shape Guide Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peripheral guide wires are commonly used in medical procedures to guide catheters through blood vessels. There are three main types of guide wires: straight guide wires, angled guide wires, and J-shape guide wires. Straight guide wires are used for straightforward procedures, angled guide wires are used for navigating around curves and corners in blood vessels, and J-shape guide wires are specifically designed to access hard-to-reach areas. Each type of guide wire serves a specific purpose in ensuring successful catheter navigation during medical procedures.</p></p>
