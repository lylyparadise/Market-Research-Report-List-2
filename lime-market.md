<p><strong>The Lime Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quick Lime</li><li>Hydrated Lime</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quick Lime, also known as Calcium Oxide, is a chemical compound used in various industrial processes such as steel manufacturing, water treatment, and agricultural applications. It is produced by heating limestone in kilns until it decomposes into lime and carbon dioxide. Hydrated Lime, also called Calcium Hydroxide, is created by adding water to Quick Lime, resulting in a highly alkaline substance. The Quick Lime market caters to industries requiring raw material for various chemical reactions, while the Hydrated Lime market serves applications that require a milder and more stable pH level.</p></p>
