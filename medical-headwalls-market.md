<p><p>Medical headwalls are essential fixtures in healthcare settings, available in two main types: horizontal and vertical market. Horizontal headwalls are designed to run along the length of patient rooms, providing easy access to medical gas outlets and electrical ports. Vertical headwalls, on the other hand, are mounted vertically on the wall and are popular in intensive care units and operating rooms. Both types aim to streamline patient care by centralizing medical equipment and facilitating efficient delivery of services.</p></p>
