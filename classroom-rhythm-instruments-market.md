<p><p>Classroom rhythm instruments market can be categorized into various types including tambourines, recorders, ocarinas, melodions, xylophones, rhythm sets/handbells, harmonicas, and others. Tambourines are percussion instruments with jingles while recorders and ocarinas are wind instruments. Melodions are similar to a keyboard harmonica, xylophones are percussion instruments with wooden bars, and rhythm sets/handbells are sets of various percussion instruments. Harmonicas produce sound by blowing air into reed chambers. Other instruments include maracas, castanets, and shakers.</p></p>
