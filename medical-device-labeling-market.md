<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive Labels</li><li>Glue Applied Labels</li><li>Sleeve Labels</li><li>In Mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical device labeling markets consist of various types of labels. Pressure sensitive labels are adhesive labels that stick to the device without the need for additional glue. Glue applied labels require an adhesive to be applied to the label for attachment. Sleeve labels wrap around the device for full coverage. In mold labels are embedded during the manufacturing process for a seamless look. Each type of labeling offers different benefits and advantages depending on the specific needs of the medical device.</p></p>
