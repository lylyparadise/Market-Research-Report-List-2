<p><strong>The Industrial Road Roller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibratory Roller</li><li>Static Road Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Road Roller market is segmented into two main types: Vibratory Rollers and Static Road Rollers. Vibratory Rollers are equipped with a vibrating drum that compacts the soil or asphalt by applying dynamic forces. They are suitable for a variety of terrain types and are more efficient in achieving a smooth finish. Static Road Rollers, on the other hand, use their weight to compress the material without any vibrations. They are ideal for compacting cohesive materials like clay or gravel. Each type has its own advantages depending on the specific construction project requirements.</p></p>
