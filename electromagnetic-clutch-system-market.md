<p><strong>The Electromagnetic Clutch System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Disk Clutch</li><li>Multi Disk Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic clutch system market consists of two main types: Single Disk Clutch and Multi Disk Clutch. Single Disk Clutch systems involve a single friction plate that engages with an electromagnetic field to transfer power, making them simpler and more cost-effective for light to medium-duty applications. In contrast, Multi Disk Clutch systems utilize multiple friction plates to handle higher torque loads, making them ideal for heavy-duty industrial machinery and vehicles. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications based on their power requirements.</p></p>
