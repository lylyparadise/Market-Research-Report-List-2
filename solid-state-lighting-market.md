<p><p>Solid State Lighting market consists of three main types: LED, OLED, and PLED. LED (Light Emitting Diode) technology is widely used for general lighting applications due to its energy efficiency and longevity. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) technology offers flexible and thin lighting solutions, suitable for applications such as displays and ambient lighting. PLED (Polymer Light Emitting Diode) technology provides lightweight, low-cost lighting options, making it ideal for signage and decorative lighting. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in the solid state lighting market.</p></p>
