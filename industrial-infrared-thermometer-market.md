<p><p>Industrial Infrared Thermometers can be categorized into two main types: Contact type and Contactless type. Contact type thermometers require physical contact with the object being measured, while Contactless infrared thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance without making direct contact with the object. Contact type thermometers are ideal for precise measurements on stationary objects, while Contactless thermometers are often used for quick and convenient temperature readings on moving or hard-to-reach objects in industrial settings.</p></p>
